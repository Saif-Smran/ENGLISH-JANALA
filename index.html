Frequently Asked Questions

1. How can I start learning English on this website?
You can start by exploring our beginner-friendly lessons, interactive exercises, and practice tests. Simply visit our homepage and choose a course that fits your level.
2. Is this website free to use?
Yes! English জানালা offers free resources for learning English. Some advanced courses or features may require a premium subscription, but most lessons are accessible for free.
3. Do I need to create an account?
You can access some lessons without an account, but creating a free account lets you track progress, save lessons, and participate in quizzes.
4. How can I build my English vocabulary?
You can improve your vocabulary by using our daily word lists, flashcards, quizzes, and interactive exercises. Reading articles and watching videos with subtitles also helps.
5. Do you offer certificates for completed courses?
Yes! After completing a course, you may receive a digital certificate as proof of completion. Check the course details to see if a certificate is available.
